Tips for Managing Availability

Recurring Time Blocks

  • • Use for regular work hours or study sessions
  • • Great for building consistent daily routines
  • • Automatically applied each week

One-time Blocks

  • • Perfect for special events or meetings
  • • Use for vacation time or blocked calendar days
  • • More flexible than recurring blocks

Pro Tip

The more availability you provide, the better the scheduling engine can organize your tasks. Try to be realistic about when you can actually focus on work.
